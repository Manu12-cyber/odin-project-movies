Under Paris

About the movie:

Under Paris is a 2024 French action horror disaster film directed by Xavier Gens, who co-wrote it with Yannick Dahan, Maud Heywang, and Yael Langman. It stars Berenice Bejo as a grieving marine biologist who is forced to face her tragic past in order to save Paris from a bloodbath when a giant shark appears in the Seine.

Produced on a budget of 19.6 million euros the film was released by Netflix on June 3, 2024 and initially recieved mixed to positive reviews, with some critics comparing it to Jaws (1975) and other films of the shark movie genre. Subsequent reviews have been less favourable.

Critical Response

On a review aggregator website Rotten Tomatoes, the films holds an approval rating of 65% based on 34 reviews, with an average rating of 6.00/10. The website's consensus reads: "Marrying enviromental themes with bloody thrills, this Gallic entry into the shark attack ultimately lands on the right side of ridiculous fun." Metacritic assigned the film a weighted average score of 57 out of 100, based on six critics, indicating "mixed or average reviews".
